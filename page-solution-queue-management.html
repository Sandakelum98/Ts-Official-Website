Token Dispenser

Queue token dispensers are among the most useful tools when it comes to managing customers. They allow employees to stop worrying about crowds and leave the check-in process entirely in their customers’ hands.

Upon entering a business venue, a customer comes to the kiosk and selects a service they need. A token dispenser then generates and prints out a paper token which contains date, branch, list of selected service and more.Token dispensers are also integrated with token display systems and digital signage. Each ticket’s number is unique, and it’s displayed on the screen when the turn is up.

Features and Benefits
  • Reduced wait times.
  • Streamlined customer flow.
  • Automates the process, giving employees more time to focus on their core responsibilities.
  • Live TV integration.
  • Reduces number of reception staff.
