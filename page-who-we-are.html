Who we are

Leaders in Digital World

Established in 2011, T S Technologies holds expertise in Systems Integration, Hospital and Virtual Queue Management Systems, Queue Management, and Digital Signage Solution. We are fully committed to our promise of excellence to develop and introduce customisable and technological adept solutions which meet our clients’ various needs.

Our Vision

“Our vision has been to provide qualified and valued products, to be professional and provide on-time services with 100% Customer Satisfaction which is our success.”

Our Mission

“We consider our product’s quality and sustainability as a warranty and reference of their further services. We see our employees as partners and our mission has always been to meet their clients’ requirements and demands in the best proper way and raise quality.”

Our Core Values

We at TS Technologies have had a mission since 2011 to provide independent, pragmatic advice to challenge existing solutions in order to accelerate and optimize every aspect of your business in various industries such as telecommunications, banks, healthcare, entertainment, and so on. We now have over 200 clients because our business strategy is focused and client-centric, with a simple approach to making your business successful. Meanwhile, we take greater pleasure in serving a large number of clients, and our commitment and responsibility to satisfy stakeholders will continue.

  • We encourage and believe in performance-driven independent services.
  • We respect innovation and creativity.
  • We uphold diversity and inclusion in our talent circle.
  • Our main goal is to delight our consumers.

Why TS Technologies

With our strategic partnerships with a number of leading industry partners, we were able to leverage vast sets of knowledge and best-in-class services.
